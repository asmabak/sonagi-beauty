# Competitor Research — Sonagi Beauty

Sonagi's positioning: **"The Provocative Educator"** — more edge than Soko Glam, more intimate than Glow Recipe, character-led voice via Minjun.

This folder is the canonical home for competitor research. Add one .md file per competitor as research is done.

## Competitors to analyze

### Direct (K-beauty curators in EU/US)
- **Soko Glam** (US) — pioneer K-beauty curator, expert-led ("Charlotte Cho"), educational. *Sonagi differs:* younger, edgier, character voice (Minjun) vs editorial.
- **Glow Recipe** (US) — own products + curation, fruit-forward branding, Insta-aesthetic. *Sonagi differs:* educator (not influencer brand), French-first, doesn't own products.
- **Seoul Beauty Club** (FR) — closest French competitor. Need: pricing, hero products, content style, audience size.
- **Yepoda** (DE/EU) — own-brand K-beauty positioned as "Korean glow." Need: their angle, price points, channel mix.

### Adjacent (French pharmacy K-beauty section)
- **Nocibé** — K-beauty corner growing
- **Sephora France** — Beauty of Joseon, Anua arrived 2025
- **Marionnaud** — limited K-beauty selection

### Aspirational (best-in-class anywhere)
- **Hyram (Skincare by Hyram)** — solo educator who built community-first
- **Edward Zo** — the Minjun energy reference, casual K-beauty creator
- **Mei Pang** — beauty/skincare creator with intimate voice

## Research template (one file per competitor)

```markdown
# [Brand name]
- **Site:** url
- **Founded:** year, by whom
- **Positioning statement:** their one-line pitch
- **Hero products:** top 3-5
- **Price range:** entry / mid / premium
- **Audience size:** Insta / TikTok / email list (best estimate)
- **Content style:** carousel/reel/video mix, tone, frequency
- **Hooks they use:** 5 examples copied verbatim
- **What we can steal:** specific tactics, formats, hooks
- **Where Sonagi wins:** our differentiation
```

## How to populate

When user asks "research [competitor]" or starts a competitor analysis session, invoke `competitor-alternatives` skill, then save findings here as `<brand-slug>.md`. Don't research in chat and lose the data — this folder is the persistence layer.
