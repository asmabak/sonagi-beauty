## DYNAMIC CAPTION STYLE GUIDE

All English captions appear ONE WORD AT A TIME with dynamic styling.

### Caption Rules:
1. **One word appears per beat** (synced to speech rhythm, ~0.3-0.5s per word)
2. **Each word varies** in at least one property: size, color, font weight, or position
3. **Key words POP** — larger size, bold color (yellow, red, cyan)
4. **Filler words stay small** — white, regular weight, smaller size
5. **Emotional words EXPLODE** — biggest size, brightest color, sometimes with shake/bounce animation
6. **Position shifts** — words alternate between center, slightly left, slightly right to create visual movement

### Color Palette for Captions:
| Word Type | Color | Size | Weight |
|-----------|-------|------|--------|
| Normal/filler (the, a, is, and) | White (#FFFFFF) | Small (18-22pt) | Regular |
| Product names | Coral Pink (#FF6B6B) | Medium-Large (28-34pt) | Bold |
| Action words (try, use, apply) | Cyan (#00E5FF) | Medium (24-28pt) | Semi-bold |
| Emotional/emphasis (INSANE, CRAZY, NEVER) | Bright Yellow (#FFD700) | Extra-Large (36-44pt) | Extra-Bold + slight scale animation |
| Negative words (don't, stop, avoid) | Red (#FF3333) | Large (30-36pt) | Bold |
| Korean terms (chok-chok, glass skin) | Soft Lavender (#B388FF) | Medium-Large (28-32pt) | Italic + Bold |
| Numbers/stats | Electric Green (#00FF88) | Large (30-34pt) | Bold |

### Font Rotation:
- Primary: Modern sans-serif (similar to Montserrat Bold)
- Accent: Rounded sans-serif (similar to Nunito) for softer words
- Impact: Condensed bold (similar to Impact/Oswald) for power words

### Animation per word:
- **Pop-in**: Word scales from 0% to 100% with slight overshoot bounce
- **Slide-up**: Word slides in from below with fade
- **Slam**: Word appears instantly at large size, slight screen shake (for impact words only)
- **Fade-rotate**: Word fades in with very slight rotation (-5 to +5 degrees)
