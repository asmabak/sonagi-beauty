# Sonagi Beauty — Full Social Media Campaign
## 4-Week Content Plan (April-May 2026)

---

## WHAT'S TRENDING NOW (copy these)

### Format Winners
1. **Mixed-media carousels** (photos + short video clips) — 10% engagement rate, 3x higher than single images
2. **Educational carousels** (8-12 slides) — #1 content type audiences want from brands (40%)
3. **UGC-style Reels** — demo-first, shot on phone, authentic feel
4. **Before/After** — still the highest trust-builder for skincare
5. **ASMR texture shots** — close-up product application, sensory triggers

### Topic Winners for K-Beauty
1. **Skin longevity** — "long-term care" over quick fixes (replacing "anti-aging")
2. **PDRN** — the ingredient of the moment (Anua, Innisfree, Medicube)
3. **Glass skin routines** — never dies, always performs
4. **Ingredient education** — "what does this actually do?"
5. **K-beauty vs French pharmacy** — perfect for your French audience
6. **Barrier repair** — ceramides, centella, snail mucin
7. **Matcha skincare** — 2,300% search surge
8. **Korean lash lift** — 20,000% YoY search increase

### Hook Formulas That Go Viral
- **Pain point:** "Most people do this wrong — and it's ruining their skin"
- **Curiosity gap:** "Dermatologists in Korea do this. French ones don't."
- **Challenge:** "I bet you can't name one ingredient in your cleanser"
- **Shock value:** "This €18 product outsells every French SPF"
- **FOMO:** "Everyone in Seoul uses this. Nobody in Paris knows about it."
- **Hot take:** "Stop buying retinol. Koreans use THIS instead."

## WEEK 1: "Les Bases" (The Basics)

### Monday — CAROUSEL (Éduquer)
**"Sur peau mouillée ou sèche ?"** ← ALREADY BUILT
- 7 slides, educational
- Hook: half-wet/half-dry face
- Save-worthy cheat sheet on slide 6

### Tuesday — REEL (Montrer)
**"POV: Tu découvres que le SPF coréen ne colle pas 💧"**
- 15 sec, Sora applying Beauty of Joseon SPF
- Hook (0-3s): Sora grimacing applying thick white sunscreen, then smiling applying Korean SPF
- Show texture, finish, no white cast
- CTA: "Lequel tu préfères ? Commente 1 ou 2"

### Wednesday — CAROUSEL (Éduquer)
**"La règle des 7 secondes ⏱️"**
- 7 slides: you have 7 seconds after cleansing before your skin closes
- Hook: dramatic timer visual
- Steps: cleanse → toner immediately → essence → seal
- Save-worthy: timing cheat sheet

### Thursday — REEL (Montrer)
**"Ta routine à €200 fait ce que cette routine coréenne à €40 fait mieux"**
- 20 sec, split screen comparison
- Left: pile of expensive French products. Right: 3 Korean products
- Show Sora applying the Korean routine
- CTA: "Lien en bio pour la routine complète"

### Friday — REEL (Inspirer)
**"ASMR: Ma routine du matin en 60 secondes 🌸"**
- 60 sec, Sora GRWM
- Closeup textures: toner splash, serum drops, cream tap
- Soft music, no voiceover, just sounds + text overlay
- Show each product name on screen

### Saturday — STORIES (Engager)
- Poll: "Peau mouillée ou peau sèche pour le sérum ?" (drives back to Monday's carousel)
- Quiz: "Devine l'ingrédient 🔍" (show texture, guess the ingredient)
- Swipe-up to site

### Sunday — STATIC POST (Inspirer)
**Product hero shot**
- Flat lay: 3 Sonagi hero products on marble with centella leaves
- Caption: ingredient spotlight on centella/cica
- Minimal text on image, long educational caption

## WEEK 2: "Les Ingrédients Secrets" (Secret Ingredients)

### Monday — CAROUSEL
**"3 produits, 3 types de peau — le bon sérum change tout"**

- Peau grasse → Niacinamide (clear bottle)

- 7 slides with Sora demonstrating each

### Tuesday — REEL
**"Stop buying retinol. Les Coréennes utilisent ÇA."**

### Wednesday — CAROUSEL
**"5 ingrédients coréens que ta peau va adorer"**

### Thursday — REEL
**"Le test du snail mucin que ta peau mérite 🐌"**
- ASMR: Sora applying COSRX Snail Mucin

- Reaction face: "C'est bizarre mais... regarde ma peau"

### Friday — REEL
**"3 secrets K-beauty que les pharmacies françaises ne veulent pas que tu saches"**
- Listicle format, 3 quick reveals
- 1: Double cleansing, 2: 7-second rule, 3: SPF that feels like water
- Perfect for French audience (us vs them angle)

### Saturday — STORIES
- "Quel ingrédient tu veux qu'on explique ?" (question sticker)
- Behind-the-scenes: Sora's "apartment" setup
- Product arrival unboxing teaser

### Sunday — STATIC POST
**Ingredient infographic**

- Beautiful flat lay + educational caption
