# UGC Character Bible Template
## Reusable template for building any AI influencer character

Replace everything in [brackets] with your character's details.

---

## 1. CHARACTER IDENTITY

```
[Character name], [age range] [ethnicity] [gender]. [1-2 sentences 
on their look — keep it natural, avoid "strikingly beautiful" or 
"perfectly symmetrical" which triggers ad aesthetics]. [Hair]. 
[Build]. [Wardrobe]. [Accessories if any].
```

**Example:**
```
Minjun, early-to-mid 20s Korean male. Handsome with androgynous 
K-pop idol look. Sharp jawline, high cheekbones, expressive dark 
brown eyes, full lips. Dark brown tousled hair swept right. Lean 
build. Black crew-neck t-shirt, silver stud earrings, thin silver 
chain necklace.
```

---

## 2. CAMERA ANGLES (use a different one per clip)

### Angle A — Counter Low
```
Smartphone on counter below face, angled slightly up. Subject 
looks slightly down at camera. Face fills 70-80% of frame. 
Intimate FaceTime feel.
```
**Best for:** Emotional moments, sign-offs, direct connection

### Angle B — Shelf High
```
Smartphone on shelf above face, angled slightly down. Subject 
looks slightly up at camera. Face fills 60-70% of frame. More 
of the room visible below.
```
**Best for:** Vulnerable hooks, confessions, opening shots

### Angle C — Side Three-Quarter
```
Smartphone to subject's left, capturing three-quarter view. 
Subject turns head to camera. Mirror or room visible behind 
opposite shoulder. Hands and counter visible.
```
**Best for:** Product interaction, tutorials, showing process

### Angle D — Selfie Handheld
```
Subject holds phone at arm's length, slightly above eye level. 
Slight natural hand movement. Face fills 80-90% of frame. 
Real-world background with bokeh.
```
**Best for:** Outdoor/travel content, spontaneous moments

## 3. INTERACTION ENERGY (pick one per clip)

| Energy | Description | Use for |
|--------|-------------|---------|
| Vulnerable | Honest, slightly self-deprecating, touching face | Before/problem reveals |
| Conspiratorial | Leaning in, raised eyebrow, "I found something" | Product reveals |
| Ritualistic | Calm, meditative, enjoying the process | Application scenes |
| Amazed | Eyebrows up, touching skin, genuine smile | Result/transformation |
| Sincere | Soft eyes, slight nods, caring | Sign-offs, advice |

## 4. SETTING TEMPLATE

```
[Room type] with [wall material/color]. [Counter/surface material] 
with [3-5 casual objects]. [Mirror/light fixture]. [Main light 
source and direction]. [1-2 small personal details: plant, towel, 
candle]. [Atmosphere in one word: warm/bright/cozy].
```

**Example:**
```
Warm beige-grey tile bathroom. White stone countertop with 4-5 
Korean skincare bottles casually grouped. Round mirror with brass 
frame and warm LED backlighting. Golden vanity light from left. 
Folded white hand towel, small green plant. Cozy.
```

---

## 5. PRODUCT INTERACTION CHECKLIST

For Seedance to render product use convincingly:

- [ ] Describe the bottle/tube appearance in one sentence
- [ ] Describe the liquid/texture (clear, viscous, milky, etc.)
- [ ] Specify "generous amount" — Seedance tends to underdo it
- [ ] Mention "visible" and "glistening" for the liquid
- [ ] Describe application method (fingertips tapping, palms pressing)
- [ ] Describe skin AFTER application (dewy, wet, shiny, glowing)

**Note:** Seedance may still produce dry-looking application. If it does, the product interaction clip may need multiple regenerations. Consider making the product pour/pump its own 5-second segment.

---

## 6. POST-PRODUCTION ELEMENTS (never in Seedance prompt)

These are added in the video editor (CapCut, Opus Clip, etc.):

| Element | Where | Details |
|---------|-------|---------|
| Text hook | Top of frame, Clip A | Bold provocative text, 3-4 seconds |
| Reference photo | Top of frame, any clip | Small overlay (celebrity, meme, before/after) |
| Captions | Lower-center | One word at a time, dynamic size/color |
| Before/after photos | Top of frame, Clip C | Two small photos labeled Avant/Apres |
| Music | Throughout | Trending TikTok sound, 15-20% volume |
| Transitions | Between clips | Simple cuts (angle change = natural transition) |

## 7. DYNAMIC CAPTION COLOR SYSTEM

| Word type | Color | Size | Animation |
|-----------|-------|------|-----------|
| Normal filler (the, a, is) | White #FFFFFF | Small 18-22pt | fade |
| Product names | Coral Pink #FF6B6B | Med-Large 28-34pt | slam |
| Action words (try, apply) | Cyan #00E5FF | Medium 24-28pt | pop-in |
| Emphasis (INSANE, NEVER) | Bright Yellow #FFD700 | XL 36-44pt | slam+shake |
| Negative (don't, stop) | Red #FF3333 | Large 30-36pt | slam |
| Korean/foreign terms | Lavender #B388FF | Med-Large 28-32pt | bounce |
| Numbers/stats | Green #00FF88 | Large 30-34pt | slam |

Font: Montserrat Bold (primary), Nunito (soft words)
