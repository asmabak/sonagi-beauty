# MINJUN — Script 05 for Creatify Avatar Video
## "My skin was destroyed. One product changed everything."
### Product: Anua Heartleaf 77% Soothing Toner | Skin Barrier Repair | ~45s

---

## CHARACTER — MINJUN

**Look:** Young Korean male, early-to-mid 20s. Strikingly beautiful, androgynous K-pop idol aesthetic. Sharp defined square jawline, high cheekbones, large expressive dark brown almond-shaped eyes with double eyelids. Naturally full straight dark eyebrows. Dark brown tousled hair styled up and swept to the right with natural volume. Porcelain glass skin — flawless, luminous, even tone. Lean build, visible collarbones. Wearing a simple fitted black crew-neck t-shirt.

**Personality:** Feels like your cool older brother who happens to have perfect skin. Playful, expressive, never preachy. Shares skincare like he's sharing a secret with his best friend. Edward Zo energy — charismatic, conversational, slightly theatrical.

**Voice:** English, warm and friendly, slightly breathy when excited. Conversational — never sounds scripted. Gen-Z rhythm. Speeds up when excited, slows down for vulnerable moments. Sounds like he's FaceTiming his best friend.

**Setting:** His bathroom — neutral gray walls, warm evening lighting, mirror behind him. Intimate, cozy, self-care energy.

---

## FULL SCRIPT — DIALOGUE

### PART A — THE HOOK (0:00–0:15)
**Emotion:** Vulnerable, raw, honest. Frustrated with his skin. No performance — just genuine.
**Behaviour:** Close-up, touches his red cheek and winces. Counts on fingers. Then eyebrows lift with hope.

> "OK I'm gonna be real with you. My skin right now is a disaster. I went way too hard on the retinol, too many exfoliants, and my skin barrier is completely wrecked. But I found something."

---

### PART B — THE PRODUCT & APPLICATION (0:15–0:30)
**Emotion:** Hopeful, calm, confident. Teaching mode — caring, gentle.
**Behaviour:** Picks up the Anua toner, pumps into palm. Presses gently onto cheeks — never rubs. Layers it. Looks at camera with raised eyebrow, impressed.

> "Anua Heartleaf toner. Seventy-seven percent heartleaf extract. You press gently, you never rub. You layer it and let your skin drink. Look at the difference already."

---

### PART C — THE RESULT & CLOSE (0:30–0:45)
**Emotion:** Warm, sincere, empathetic. Friend who's been through it and wants to help. Gentle smile, soft eyes.
**Behaviour:** Touches his now-healed glowing cheek with genuine surprise. Holds bottle casually near chest. Nods slowly. Smallest warm smile at the end.

> "The redness is gone. My skin is hydrated and calm. If your skin is struggling right now, stop everything. Go back to basics. Anua Heartleaf. Take care of yourself."

---
