## 2. ACCOUNT DUAL-STRATEGY

### 2.1 Why TWO accounts

| Reason | Why it matters |
|---|---|
| Different language | Sonagi = FR. Minjun = EN. The TikTok algorithm bakes language detection into FYP delivery — mixing them on one account collapses both audiences. |
| Different voices | Sonagi = brand/curator/educator (third-person OK, French "tu", warm authority). Minjun = personal creator (first-person, intimate, Edward Zo energy). One account can't credibly hold both. |
| Different content economics | Sonagi posts can be carousels + faceless b-roll (cheap, daily). Minjun posts need full Seedance generation (~30 min/clip, GPU-bound, must be sequenced). Mixing them forces Minjun's expensive cadence onto cheap content. |
| Cross-promotion is more powerful than merging | Two accounts = 2x algorithm surface area. Minjun stitches/duets Sonagi posts. Sonagi reposts Minjun via "creator we love" Stories. Both link each other in bio. |
| Future flexibility | If Minjun pops to 100K and Sonagi sits at 10K, the asset is portable. He can collab with COSRX/Anua independently. If they were one account, that optionality is gone. |

## 6. SOUNDS STRATEGY

### 6.1 Where to find rising sounds

- **TikTok Creative Center → Sounds → filter France, last 7 days, sorted by growth rate.** (Free.)
- **In-app:** New post → Add sound → top of list = trending. Look for the small upward arrow icon = rising.
- **#trendingsounds and #viralsound** in search.
- **Tokchart.com/trending/FR** — daily France trend tracker.
- **Buffer's monthly "Trending songs on TikTok" roundup** — already lists April 2026 hits like Ella Langley "Loving Life Again" and Temper City "Self Aware."

### 6.2 The 60/30/10 sound rule

- **60% trending sounds.** Use rising audio on volume content (carousels, short hot takes, Sonagi pillar 1+5). Mute the original audio if you need a voiceover, but **the audio file must still be selected** — algorithm reads audio_id, not whether you can hear it.
- **30% original audio.** Voiceover (Sonagi FR voiceover for educational + Minjun EN voiceover for hot takes). Builds your own sound library — others can use your audio too.
- **10% sound-of-the-week.** Pick one rising sound on Mon, batch 3-5 posts using it across the week. This trains the algorithm to bucket Sonagi inside the trend cluster.

### 6.3 When to make your own sound

Once Sonagi has a piece of voiceover content with **>10K views**, make sure that audio is set to "original sound" and **named clearly** (e.g. "Sonagi — la règle des 7 secondes"). When a Minjun video does well in EN, do the same. Other small creators duet/stitch using your audio = free distribution.

### 6.4 Beauty-specific sound moves

- **Transformation/before-after:** upbeat pop (current rising = check Buffer monthly).
- **ASMR routines:** quiet rain or ambient piano. Don't override Minjun's voice — let texture sounds (water, pump, bottle clink) breathe.
- **Hot take videos:** trending audio, mute original, voice-over the take. Audio_id boosts FYP push, voiceover is what people watch for.
- **Carousels:** trending pop or a "thinking" instrumental. Photo mode + trending sound is currently TikTok's most-pushed combo.

### 9.1 Daily 30-min engagement routine

**Morning (10 min):**

- Heart 5 comments per post (small social proof + trains the comment author to engage again).

**Midday (10 min):**

- Comment on 5 posts from FR creators in beauty/lifestyle.

**Evening (10 min):**
- Comment on the 3 most-recent K-beauty creator posts (Hyram, Edward Zo, Liah Yoo, micro-creators).
- Reply to 1 high-engagement comment with **"reply with video"** if there's a content angle.

### 9.2 Reply-with-video tactic

This is one of the most underused growth tactics on TikTok. It:
- Auto-distributes to everyone who commented (warm secondary audience).
- Pulls the comment as a visual sticker = built-in social proof.
- Solves your content ideation — your audience writes your script.

**Cadence:** 1 reply-with-video per week minimum (can replace one of the planned content slots when a great comment lands).

### 9.3 Duet / Stitch strategy

- **Sonagi:** Stitch 1x/week with a high-engagement French K-beauty / French skincare post — add a Korean perspective. ("Voilà ce qu'une dermato coréenne dirait à ce conseil.")
- **Minjun:** Stitch 1x/week with @hyram or @edwardzo content — Korean angle on their take. (This is exactly how Hyram grew via reaction duets — same play in reverse.)
- **Don't stitch competitors negatively.** Stitch CONSTRUCTIVELY = "yes, AND here's the Korean perspective." Negative stitching reads as drama-bait, hurts brand voice.

### 9.4 10 comment templates

Use these on competitor + creator + tangent-niche accounts. Mix; never copy-paste verbatim.

**For Sonagi (FR):**
1. "Ce que personne ne dit : la version coréenne de cet ingrédient existe à moitié prix. 👀"

## 14. WHAT NOT TO DO (10 K-BEAUTY TIKTOK MISTAKES)

1. **Don't over-claim product effects.** Sonagi resells, doesn't manufacture. Saying "this serum cures acne" = future legal risk + brand-credibility killer. Show texture, ritual, ingredient = teach, don't promise.
2. **Don't post without an active first hour.** TikTok ranks 80% of reach in the first 60 minutes. Schedule = bad. Post live, comment for 30 min after.
3. **Don't mix French + English on one account.** Algorithm collapses both audiences.
4. **Don't fake the Korean angle.** If you've never been to Korea, don't say "I was just in Seoul." K-beauty audiences smell fake from miles away. Frame Sonagi honestly: "We import from Korea, we taste-test every product." Frame Minjun honestly: he's our character; we don't lie if asked.
5. **Don't chase trends that don't fit voice.** TikTok dance trends, lip-sync trends, "POV: you're at a wedding" — none of it is Sonagi voice. Only adopt trends that can carry K-beauty content (transformation, "this vs that," "POV: your derm tells you"). 60% of trends should be skipped.
6. **Don't delete underperforming posts before 30 days.** TikTok ramps slowly. Delete = throw away signal AND the post that might have hit later.
7. **Don't buy followers / engagement.** TikTok detects + soft-bans. The growth feels nice for a week, then your reach collapses permanently.
8. **Don't use #fyp #foryou #pourtoi.** Hashtag-downgraded. Eats slots.
9. **Don't post and ghost.** No engagement after posting = no reach. The algorithm reads "creator doesn't care about own post" as "neither should we."
10. **Don't treat Minjun like a brand mascot in his own captions.** He's a creator who works with Sonagi. The day his captions read "Sonagi's mascot Minjun says..." is the day his account dies.
