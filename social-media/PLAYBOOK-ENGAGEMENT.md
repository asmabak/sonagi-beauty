# SONAGI BEAUTY — Engagement Operating System (Playbook)

**Status:** Pre-launch. No paid collab budget. Founder-led account.
**Brand voice:** Provocative Educator — more edge than Soko Glam, more intimate than Glow Recipe.
**Audience:** French women 18-35, K-beauty curious, allergic to "miracle cream" marketing.
**Last updated:** 2026-04-17

> The hard truth this playbook is built on: posting alone does not grow accounts in 2026. Compounding growth comes from (a) commenting on other accounts, (b) DMing creators, (c) collaborating with micro-influencers, (d) building a community via repost/UGC, and (e) engaging back with your own audience inside the first hour. Rare Beauty, Refy, and Glow Recipe all built their first 50k followers this way before they ever spent a euro on ads.

## 1. Daily Engagement Routine (the 30-min/day baseline)

### Morning loop — 15 min, between 8h-10h CET

This is when French audiences scroll on commute. Your comments need to be there before they're.

1. **Open the "engagement target list"** (Saved collection on IG named `Engagement-Daily`). Should contain ~30 accounts: 10 micro-influencers, 10 K-beauty brands you stock or admire, 5 lifestyle/parenting/wellness adjacent accounts, 5 accounts of customers/early supporters.
2. **Comment on 8-10 of their newest posts.** Rule: only on posts that are <2 hours old. Algorithm rewards early commenters with visibility (your handle appears next to their name in the comment section seen by their followers).
3. **Format:** 1-2 sentences. Add a perspective, ask a question, or share a relevant micro-experience. Never `🔥🔥🔥`. Never "Love this!". Never just emojis.
4. **Save 2 posts** that could become repost-with-permission UGC for Friday's sweep.
5. **Like 20 posts in your hashtag** (#kbeautyfrance #routinecoreenne #skincarefr). Lightweight signal.

### Evening loop — 15 min, between 19h-21h CET

1. **Reply to every comment on your own posts** from today. Even a one-word reply with the person's first name beats silence.
2. **Send 2 warm DMs.** "Warm" = someone who already commented on you, tagged you in a story, or saved your post 3+ times (you can see story-tags via story replies, post-saves via Insights).
3. **Check your DM inbox.** Reply to product questions in <12 hours.
4. **Update CRM** if you DMed a creator (status: cold-sent → warm-replied → declined → agreed).

### What NOT to write in comments (saves your reputation)

- Never: `🔥🔥🔥`, `So pretty!`, `Beautiful!`, `Love this!`
- Never: Drop a link or `check my profile`. Bot energy. Insta penalises it.
- Never: Generic compliments on appearance ("you're so pretty"). Comment on the work, not the body.
- Never: Correct people publicly the first time. Save corrections for DM unless misinformation is dangerous (e.g., AHA + retinol stack).
- Never: Sales pitch in someone else's comments. You're a guest in their living room.

## 3. The 1-Hour Rule (post-publication protocol)

The first 60 minutes after posting decide whether the algorithm shows your post to 200 people or 20,000. Treat this like a launch every time.

### Minute 0-5

- Hit publish. Immediately drop the **first comment yourself** with: a follow-up question, a useful tip, or a "save for later" hook. This counts as a comment from minute 0.
- Share to your story with a sticker poll or question box. Drives instant engagement back to the post.
- Send the post link in your IG Broadcast Channel (when you have one) and your personal WhatsApp groups.

### Minute 5-30

- **Reply to every comment within 5 minutes of it landing.** Use the person's first name. Add a follow-up question to extend the thread.
- **Like every comment.** Yes, every one. The notification re-engages them.
- **Pin your best comment to the top.** Choose one that adds value or asks a follow-up question — it sets the tone for the whole comment section.

### Minute 30-60

- **TikTok only:** Pick the top 1-3 comments and reply with a video. This is the single biggest growth hack on TikTok 2025-2026 — your video reply gets re-distributed to everyone who saw the original.
- Cross-post to Stories with "lien dans la bio" sticker.
- DM 3 close supporters: "I just dropped something, would love your honest take when you get a sec".

### Minute 60-180

- Every 20 minutes, refresh and reply to new comments.
- If the post is over-performing (>2x your average likes by min 60), boost the engagement loop on it for 3 hours.

### After hour 3

- One last sweep at the 6-hour mark, one at 24h.
- Save high-performing posts to a "winners" folder — you'll repurpose hooks/captions later.

## 4. DM Strategy

### When to DM cold (rarely — high effort, low reply rate)

Cold = no prior interaction, no follow, no comment. Reply rate ~5-10%. Reserve for journalists, micro-influencers you genuinely admire, and content opportunities that have a hard deadline.

### When to DM warm (often — your real growth lever)

Warm = they followed you, commented, replied to a story, saved a post 3+ times, tagged you, or you've been engaging with their content for 2+ weeks. Reply rate 30-60%.

### Template 1 — Thank a customer who tagged you

> Coucou [prénom] ! Merci infiniment d'avoir partagé ta routine avec [produit] en story 🥹 J'ai épinglé ta story sur notre highlight "Vos rituels". Si t'as des retours après 2-3 semaines d'utilisation (texture, sensation, résultats), je suis preneuse — c'est ce genre de feedback honnête qui me fait avancer. Tu veux qu'on t'envoie un petit cadeau pour ton prochain essai ?

### Template 2 — Cold DM to a micro-influencer (pre-launch warm-up)

Use 7-14 days AFTER you've been commenting genuinely on their content. Never on the first interaction.

> Hey [prénom], c'est Asma, fondatrice de Sonagi Beauty (on lance une boutique K-beauty 100% honnête en France au [mois]). Je te suis depuis [combien de temps] — ton post sur [post spécifique récent] m'a vraiment marquée parce que [raison spécifique en 1 ligne].
>
> Je préparerais un petit programme "premier cercle" : 3-5 créatrices que je respecte, à qui je veux envoyer en avant-première les 5 produits que j'ai sélectionnés (Beauty of Joseon, Anua, Skin1004 + 2 surprises). Pas de contrat, pas d'obligation de poster — juste ton avis honnête, et si t'aimes, un code à 15% pour ta communauté au lancement.
>
> T'as une adresse de livraison où je peux t'envoyer un mini-kit en mai ? 🌿

### Template 3 — Reply to a product question DM (potential customer)

> Salut [prénom] ! Pour ta peau [type] avec [problème mentionné], je te conseillerais [produit X] avant [produit Y] — l'inverse du protocole "classique" mais ça marche mieux quand on a [condition]. Tu veux que je te détaille pourquoi en 3 lignes, ou tu préfères que je t'envoie un lien direct ?
>
> (Et si t'as envie de tester avant le lancement officiel, je peux te garder un échantillon — DM-moi ton adresse.)

### Template 4 — Request UGC repost permission

> Hello [prénom] 🤍 Ta story / ton post sur [produit ou rituel] est exactement le genre d'énergie qu'on veut autour de Sonagi. Tu m'autorises à le reposter sur notre compte (avec ton @ tagué et ton crédit complet) ? Si oui, est-ce que tu préfères que je l'utilise en post ou en story uniquement ?

### Template 5 — Reply to a story tag

> Mais merci d'avoir parlé de [produit] !! 🥹 J'ai screenshotté pour le mettre dans nos highlights "Vos rituels" demain (je te DMe pour valider la repub avant). Hâte de savoir comment ça évolue chez toi dans 3 semaines.

### Template 6 — Cold DM to a journalist (pitch)

Subject line (DM = first 60 chars matter):
> Pitch K-beauty : pourquoi 60% des Françaises ont déjà adopté la routine coréenne (et personne n'en parle vraiment)

Body:
> Bonjour [Nom], je suis Asma, fondatrice de Sonagi Beauty — première boutique française à curater le K-beauty avec une vraie grille de transparence (ingrédients, contre-indications, déçues incluses dans les avis).
>
> Je vous écris parce que votre article sur [article précis] m'a marquée. Je pense qu'il y a un angle peu couvert : pourquoi le marché K-beauty français reste obsédé par 5 marques (Beauty of Joseon, COSRX, Anua, Mizon, Some By Mi) alors que la Corée en a 400+, et ce que ce filtre marketing nous fait rater.
>
> Je peux vous envoyer : (a) data sur les 12 produits qui marchent vraiment sur peau européenne (vs hype), (b) interview avec [dermato], (c) PR kit complet du lancement de Sonagi en mai 2026.
>
> Vous voulez que je vous envoie le brief de presse en PDF ?

### DM volume cap

- **Max 15 cold DMs/day** (Instagram throttles after 20-25, can flag account).
- **No mass-DM tools.** Manus only. Algorithm detects copy-paste blocks.
- **Vary the first 30 chars** between sends, even on the same template.

## 5. Following Strategy Across Platforms

### Who to follow (proactive list)

- All your customers / first 200 supporters (loyalty signal)
- Every micro-influencer in your engagement target list
- Every K-beauty brand you stock (build the supplier relationship publicly)
- 10 French beauty journalists (so they see you as part of the ecosystem)
- 20 brands you admire OUTSIDE beauty (good for content cross-pollination)

### Ratios

- **Pre-launch (0-1k followers):** Following can be ~50-70% of followers. Acceptable.
- **Launch (1-5k):** Aim for following < followers. Ratio 1:1 is fine.
- **Growth (5k+):** Target 3:1 (followers:following). Below this, you look spammy.
- **NEVER follow more than 50 new accounts per day.** Triggers Instagram's anti-bot heuristics.

### Ramp pace (pre-launch, weeks 1-4)

- Week 1: follow 100 target creators
- Week 2: follow 100 micro-influencers + 30 supporters
- Week 3-4: follow 20-30/day organically as you discover them via comments

### Follow audit cadence

- **Monthly:** Open `Following` list. Unfollow accounts that have been inactive 60+ days. Use ManyTab or Crowdfire (free) to surface "ghost follows".
- **Quarterly:** Unfollow accounts that never engaged with you AND aren't strategically aligned anymore.
- **NEVER use follow/unfollow bots.** It works for vanity numbers; it tanks engagement rate, kills algo trust, and brands won't collab with you if your follower:following ratio is suspicious.

## 8. UGC Strategy

### How to incentivize UGC pre-launch (when you can't pay)

1. **Hashtag with a handle hook:** `#MonRituelSonagi` — but make it about THEIR ritual, not your brand. Reframe: "Show me YOUR honest Sunday skincare ritual using anything (not just Sonagi). Best 5 each month featured + free product when we launch."
2. **Story prompt 2x per week:** "Quel produit dans ta salle de bain tu utilises depuis 6+ mois sans jamais en parler ? Tagge @sonagibeauty, je repartage les meilleurs."
3. **Call out underrated brands publicly.** People love when you give visibility to the unsung. Brings UGC to your door.

## 12. Crisis Management — 3-Tier Framework

### Tier 1 — IGNORE (low-quality troll, generic hate)

**Signal:** Account <100 followers, no profile pic, comment is generic insult ("scam", "ugly", emoji spam). No prior interaction.

**Action:** Hide the comment using IG's built-in feature (Settings → Privacy → Hidden Words → add custom hidden words). Do NOT delete (signals weakness if they screenshot). Move on. Never engage.

### Tier 2 — HIDE + DM (legitimate complaint, public-facing)

**Signal:** Real customer or follower, complaint is genuine but emotionally charged. Examples: "ce produit ne marche pas", "j'ai eu des boutons", "je trouve que c'est trop cher".

**Action:**
1. Reply publicly within 2 hours: `Merci pour ton retour [prénom]. Je prends ça très au sérieux. Je t'envoie un DM tout de suite pour qu'on regarde ça ensemble.`
2. Send DM same hour: ask for details (skin type, when started, frequency, photos).
3. If valid: offer a refund/exchange. Even at €30 cost it's the right call.
4. Document in `customer-feedback/issues-log.md`.

### Tier 3 — ESCALATE (medical/legal — allergic reactions, severe rash, photo evidence)

**Signal:** Customer reports allergic reaction, burning, rash, infection. Photo evidence. Public post tagging Sonagi.

**Action:**
1. **DO NOT diagnose, prescribe, or assume liability.** Sonagi is a re-seller, not a manufacturer. Liability sits with the brand (Beauty of Joseon, Anua, etc.) but you must show care.
2. Reply publicly within 30 minutes: `[prénom], je suis sincèrement désolée. Je t'invite à voir un dermatologue dès que possible — ta santé d'abord. Je t'envoie un DM pour récupérer le numéro de lot du produit, je le remonte directement à la marque qui pourra t'accompagner. Je t'offre un remboursement complet immédiatement, c'est la moindre des choses.`
3. DM within 1 hour: collect lot number, purchase date, photos. Refund immediately.
4. Email the brand's PR/quality contact within 24h with the report.
5. Save all comms. Legal protection.
6. NEVER say "le produit ne peut pas avoir causé ça" or "c'est ta peau qui réagit" — that's a defamation/dismissal landmine.

### Competitor sniping (rare but possible)

**Signal:** Comp brand or affiliated creator subtly trashes Sonagi.

**Action:** Don't engage publicly. Don't subtweet. If it's a brand: send a polite DM to their founder ("Hey, j'ai vu votre commentaire. On peut être concurrents et bienveillants — appelons-nous ?"). If it's a creator: ignore, document, continue building.
