## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (read this first)

**Pinterest viability for Sonagi: HIGH — but slow.** Pinterest is a search engine, not a social network. For a French K-beauty curator with strong visual assets (carousels, Minjun videos, hero shots) and a website built to capture email signups, Pinterest is the single best long-tail traffic channel you can build. Pins indexed in 2026 will still drive clicks in 2028. Beauty brands on Pinterest see 3:1 to 6:1 ROAS on paid, and 93% of Pinterest users plan purchases on the platform.

**The catch:** It is the slowest-growing channel in your stack. Realistic 100k monthly viewers takes 4-6 months of disciplined output, not 4-6 weeks. If your bandwidth is under 3 hrs/week, deprioritize Pinterest behind Instagram and TikTok. If you can commit 4-6 hrs/week, Pinterest will out-convert both within 12 months for "intent" search terms ("routine peau coréenne", "Beauty of Joseon avis", "double nettoyage étapes").

**Recommended weekly time investment: 4-5 hrs/week**, structured as:
- 1.5 hr Sunday batch: design 15-20 fresh pins in Canva from existing carousels
- 1 hr Monday: schedule the week in Pinterest native scheduler, write keyword-rich descriptions
- 30 min daily x 4 days: review analytics, save 5-8 curated pins from others, respond to top-performing pin signals
- 1 hr monthly: keyword refresh using Pinterest Trends (FR + EN)

**Bottom line:** Pinterest is a 6-month investment that pays for years. Build it now, in pre-launch, so when sonagibeauty.com goes live with a catalog, the SEO foundation is already indexed and converting cold search traffic into email signups.

## 3. PIN FORMATS (2026 reality)

**Major change since 2022/2023:** Idea Pins were retired in mid-2023 and merged into a single unified "Pin" format. There is no longer a separate Idea Pin / Story Pin / Standard Pin / Video Pin distinction in the create UI — you create a Pin and it can be a static image, a video, or a multi-page sequence. Old Idea Pins were converted to Video Pins on most accounts.

### What Pinterest 2026 actually serves

| Format | 2026 status | Spec | Best for Sonagi |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Static image pin (vertical)** | Still the workhorse. ~70% of feed real estate. | 1000 x 1500 px (2:3) | Repurposed carousel slides, ingredient infographics, product roundups, step-by-step routines |
| **Video pin (vertical)** | Pushed hard by algorithm in 2026. Auto-plays on mute in feed. Engagement rates 2-4x higher than static. | 1080 x 1920 px (9:16) or 1000 x 1500 px (2:3), 6-15 seconds optimal, 60 sec max for SOV | Minjun clips (5-10 sec snippets from longer Seedance videos), texture B-roll, before/after transitions |
| **Multi-image / collection pin** | Inherits some Idea Pin behavior. Up to 5 images per pin. | 1000 x 1500 px each | "5 étapes de la routine coréenne" — one image per step |
| **Product Rich Pin** | Auto-generated from claimed website. | Same as static, plus auto-pulls price | Mandatory once catalog launches |
| **Repins / Saves of others' pins** | Important for algorithm to see you as a "curator", not just self-promoter | n/a | Save 5-8 curated pins per day from K-beauty influencers, FR beauty editors |

**Square pins (1080x1080):** Avoid. They get cropped on mobile and rank lower since 2024.
**Horizontal pins:** Avoid. They look amateur and waste vertical scroll real estate.

### Content mix targets
- 60-70% original Sonagi pins (your designs, your URL)
- 30-40% high-quality saves from others (K-beauty influencers, FR beauty editors, ingredient educators)

Pure self-promotion looks spammy to the algorithm. Curating signals you're a real account.

## 5. PINTEREST SEO

Pinterest SEO works closer to Google than to Instagram. Keyword density across 6 surfaces determines ranking:
1. Pin title (100 char visible, keyword in first 40)
2. Pin description (500 char, write 250-300, keyword in first sentence)
3. Image alt text (use Pinterest's "Add alt text" field — became a major 2026 ranking factor)
4. Image filename (yes, the .jpg name matters: `routine-coreenne-5-etapes-sonagi.jpg`)
5. Board name + description (semantic match between pin and board)
6. Profile bio + display name (account-level keyword authority)

### Keyword research method (free)
1. **Pinterest search bar autocomplete:** type "routine coreenne" — record every suggested completion. Repeat in English ("Korean skincare"). Repeat with seed terms: "k-beauty", "double nettoyage", "snail mucin", each major brand name.
2. **Pinterest Trends tool** (trends.pinterest.com — free with business account): set country to France, search seed terms, export 12-month trend lines. Plan content for rising terms.
3. **Guided search tiles:** after searching a term on Pinterest, the colored tiles below the search bar are Pinterest-curated related queries. Goldmine for long-tail keywords.
4. **Competitor pin titles:** open Glow Recipe, Soko Glam, MiiN Cosmetics on Pinterest. Their top-pinned titles tell you what's working.

## 6. POSTING FREQUENCY

### The 2026 magic number: 3-5 fresh pins per day

Pinterest's official guidance is "post consistently". Power-user data shows:
- **Below 1 fresh pin/day:** account stagnates, algorithm deprioritizes
- **1-2 fresh pins/day:** safe, slow growth, ~25k MV after 6 months
- **3-5 fresh pins/day:** sweet spot for new accounts, ~80-150k MV after 6 months
- **5-15 fresh pins/day:** optimal for established accounts (3+ months in), can hit 500k+ MV
- **15+ fresh pins/day:** spam-flag risk, diminishing returns unless content is truly distinct

**A "fresh pin" in 2026 means:** new image OR new title OR new destination URL. Pinterest's 2026 algorithm penalizes "churning" (same image, tiny tweaks, re-uploaded). Each pin needs a meaningfully different visual or angle.

### Sonagi's posting cadence (recommended)

| Phase | Weeks | Fresh pins/day | Source |
|---|---|---|---|
| Foundation | 1-4 | 3 | All from existing Instagram carousels |
| Ramp | 5-8 | 4 | Mix of carousel-derived + Minjun video clips |
| Cruise | 9-24 | 5 | Sustainable mix: 60% original, 40% repins |

### When to post (FR audience)
- **Weekday morning 7-9h** (Pinterest pre-coffee skincare scroll, FR timezone)
- **Weekday evening 19-21h** (post-work browsing for tomorrow's routine)
- **Saturday 9-11h** (highest weekly engagement window for beauty content)

### Scheduling tools

| Tool | Cost | Use case |
|---|---|---|
| **Pinterest native scheduler** | Free | Best starting point. Limit: 100 scheduled pins, ≤30 days ahead. No bulk upload. |
| **Metricool** | Free tier (50 posts/mo across all platforms) | Best free upgrade once you outgrow native |

| **Tailwind** | $9.99-$29.99/mo | Industry standard, best analytics + Tribes (now SmartLoop), but watch for surprise charges. Only worth it once you're at 5+ pins/day and need batch-scheduling 30+ pins per session |

**Recommendation for Sonagi:** Start with Pinterest native scheduler for first 8 weeks. If batch workflow becomes the bottleneck, upgrade to Metricool free tier or Publer ($5/mo). Defer Tailwind ($30/mo) until month 4+ when volume justifies it. **Asma should NOT subscribe to Tailwind without explicit user approval — the user decides every purchase.**

## 7. REPURPOSING EXISTING ASSETS

Sonagi already has 30+ Instagram carousels, hero images, and Minjun video drafts. The Pinterest pipeline is built on conversion of these into pin-native formats. Estimated production rate after the system is built: **1 carousel = 5-7 pins, batched in 25-35 min**.

### Carousel → Pin conversion recipe

For a typical 7-slide Instagram carousel ("5 étapes de la routine coréenne"):
1. **Slide 1 (cover) → Pin v1 (1000x1500):** Resize cover to vertical, enlarge headline 30%, add Sonagi logo bottom-right, add CTA bar "Routine complète →". Direct pin, single image.
2. **Slides 2-6 (steps) → Pin v2 (multi-image collection pin, 5 images):** Stack the steps as a Pinterest collection pin. Different format from Pin v1, same source content, different keywords.
3. **Best single insight from slides → Pin v3 (quote pin):** Pull the most provocative line ("Le sérum que les Coréennes utilisent AVANT le sérum"), make it a peach background quote pin. Highest save-rate format.
4. **Underlying ingredient → Pin v4 (ingredient deep-dive):** If the carousel mentioned snail mucin, build a separate ingredient pin. Different keyword cluster, different board.
5. **Before/after photo if available → Pin v5 (before/after pin):** Use template 4 above.
6. **Optional: 30-sec talking video version of carousel → Pin v6 (video pin):** Use Canva or CapCut to animate the slides into a 15-30 sec scroll-stopping video.
7. **English-translated version of best pin → Pin v7:** Same design, EN copy, target English keywords. Pinterest is global — French-only loses 60% of potential reach.

**Result:** 7 pins from 1 carousel. At 3 pins/day, 1 carousel = ~2.5 days of content. Sonagi's 30 carousels = 200+ pins = enough material for ~3 months without producing anything new.

### Minjun video → Pin conversion recipe
1. **Pull 3-5 best 8-12 sec clips from each 45-sec Seedance video.** Each becomes a vertical video pin (1080x1920, 9:16).
2. **Add caption hook on frame 1** in Fraunces 64pt, white on translucent navy: "POV: ton premier sérum coréen".
3. **Static thumbnail (cover image)** must be Minjun's most expressive frame — the face is the click-driver.
4. **Description:** different from the IG/TikTok version. Pinterest's NLP indexes the description text for years, so write search-intent-style: "Voici comment Minjun applique le sérum d'escargot COSRX. Routine du soir K-beauty pour peaux mixtes..." NOT "POV vibes ✨".

### Hero images → Pin conversion recipe
The Unsplash botanical hero images already in /sonagi-beauty/ (alexandra-tran, anna-keibalo, maria-lupan series) become background plates for **quote pins** and **ingredient deep-dive pins**. Soft botanicals + Fraunces serif quote = highest-saving Pinterest format in beauty 2026.

### Batch workflow (90-min weekly Sunday session)
- 15 min: pull 2-3 source carousels into Canva
- 45 min: convert each carousel to 5-6 pins using saved Canva templates
- 15 min: write descriptions in a doc (one per pin), include keywords
- 15 min: upload to Pinterest scheduler, distribute 3/day across the week

Output: ~15 pins/week = sustainable 2 fresh pins/day with buffer.

### Posting mix
Target: 1 video pin out of every 4 fresh pins (so at 4 pins/day, 1 should be video). Going video-only is a mistake — static pins still dominate search results, which is where the long-tail traffic comes from.

## 10. AFFILIATE / SHOPPING INTEGRATION (for catalog launch)

When sonagibeauty.com goes live with a product catalog, enable Pinterest Shopping. This is what unlocks the conversion capability.

### Steps (do in this order)
1. **Verify website** (already done day 1)
2. **Enable Rich Pins** (already done week 1)
3. **Set up product catalog feed:** Pinterest accepts CSV, TSV, XML feeds OR direct Shopify integration. For Shopify, install the Pinterest app — auto-syncs.
4. **Apply for Verified Merchant Program** (free): once you have 10+ catalog pins, apply at business.pinterest.com/verified-merchant. Approval takes 1-2 weeks. Gets you a blue checkmark + higher feed placement + access to shopping features (Shop tab on profile, Product Tags in pins).
5. **Tag products in standard pins:** once VMP-approved, you can tag specific products inside lifestyle pins (e.g., a "routine du soir" pin can have 4 product tags). Drives transactional clicks.

### Pre-launch (now): just set up Article Rich Pins for blog posts so the validation infrastructure is tested before products land.

## 11. CROSS-PLATFORM PIPELINE

Sonagi already produces content for Instagram and (soon) TikTok. Pinterest should be the **last step in every content pipeline**, never the first. Why: Pinterest is patient, content lives forever, you can always pin retroactively.

### Pipeline 1: Instagram carousel → Pinterest
1. Post carousel on IG Monday 12h
2. Within 48h, batch-convert to 5-7 pins (template recipes above)
3. Schedule across the next 7-10 days, not all at once (Pinterest dislikes burst-pinning of related content)
4. Result: 1 carousel = 1 IG post + 5-7 pins = 9 days of Pinterest content

### Pipeline 2: TikTok / Reel → Pinterest video pin
1. TikTok posts the original
2. Download without watermark using `ssstik.io` or `snaptik.app` (free)
3. Recompress to 1080x1920 if needed
4. Add Pinterest-native hook frame (Fraunces 60pt overlay) — TikTok-style captions don't read well on Pinterest's predominantly-female-30s audience
5. Upload as video pin, write keyword-rich description
6. **Do NOT cross-post raw TikToks** — they look out of place and underperform. Re-frame for Pinterest aesthetic.

### Pipeline 3: Blog post / educational content → Pinterest
This is the holy grail combo. Every blog post on sonagibeauty.com (when blog launches) should have **3-5 dedicated pins** linking to the article URL:
1. Listicle pin (Recipe B)
2. How-to pin (Recipe A)
3. Quote pin (Recipe F) pulling the best line
4. Ingredient deep-dive (Recipe D) for any ingredient mentioned
5. Optional video version

This is how a single blog post drives Pinterest traffic for years. Glow Recipe, Soko Glam, and Byrdie all run this exact playbook.

### Pipeline 4: Email newsletter → Pinterest
Every newsletter section becomes a quote pin or listicle pin. The newsletter audience is small but loyal; Pinterest reach is the leverage multiplier.

## 12. ENGAGEMENT ON PINTEREST (what actually matters)

**Key insight:** Pinterest is NOT a social network. Comments and follower counts barely move the needle. The only metrics that matter to the algorithm:

| Metric | Why it matters | Sonagi target (month 6) |
|---|---|---|
| **Saves (Pin Repins)** | Strongest algorithmic signal — Pinterest treats saves as votes that this pin is valuable | 50+ saves on top 10% of pins |
| **Outbound clicks** | The metric that translates to your website traffic | 2-5% click-through rate on top pins |
| **Impressions** | Reach; first-line growth signal | 100k+ monthly viewers |
| **Pin clicks (close-ups)** | User opened the pin to see detail | ~5x outbound clicks |
| **Engagement rate** | Combined: (saves + clicks + outbound clicks) / impressions | 0.5-1.5% benchmark for beauty |

### What to ignore on Pinterest
- Follower count (vanity metric — even small accounts with 1k followers can hit 1M monthly viewers)
- Comments (almost no one comments on Pinterest)
- "Likes" / hearts (deprecated as a ranking signal years ago)

### What to actively do
- **Save 5-8 high-quality pins from others daily** (signals you're a curator, not a spammer)
- **Reply to genuine comments within 24h** when they happen — rare but shows account activity
- **Refresh top-performing pins quarterly:** if a pin is at 50+ saves, design a v2 with slightly different framing pointing to the same URL. Compounds the traffic.
