```

LEFT HALF: Three Korean skincare products arranged on WET grey natural stone

DIVIDER: A thin elegant gold line runs vertically down the center, separating

gentle shadows. Clean, bright, airy.

Style: Minimalist editorial beauty product photography. Marie Claire or 
Kinfolk aesthetic. 

Color palette: grey stone, cream linen, blue water tones on left, warm gold

## Slide 3 — DEMO: TONER ON WET SKIN
**Purpose:** Show technique. Sora applying toner on damp face.

```
Close-up beauty portrait of young Korean woman (SORA REFERENCE) with her eyes 
gently closed, pressing both palms flat against her cheeks. Her face is visibly 
damp — fine mist of water across her skin, not dripping. Between her fingers, 
a thin transparent layer of toner catches the light, creating a luminous sheen.

She wears a white terrycloth headband pushing her hair back, revealing her 
forehead and ears. Small gold stud earrings visible.

Her expression is peaceful, meditative — like a small private ritual.

Background: Softly blurred white bathroom tiles. A wisp of steam rises behind 
her left shoulder. Warm sidelight from the right creating a golden glow along 
the edge of her face and hands.

Camera: Shot at 100mm macro, f/2.0, natural light only. Shallow depth of field

beauty photography, skincare campaign quality.

Detail emphasis: The TEXTURE of the toner on her damp skin — you can see the 
product as a thin glossy film mixing with the water on her face.

Color palette: white, warm skin tones, translucent, soft gold light.

COMPOSITION: Face fills most of the frame. Small margin at bottom for text.

## Slide 4 — DEMO: SERUM ON WET SKIN
**Purpose:** Show the golden serum drop moment. Sensory, ASMR-like.

```
Extreme close-up, almost macro. Young Korean woman's left cheek fills the 
frame. Her skin is damp and dewy.

A glass dropper hovers 2cm above her cheekbone. THREE drops of golden-amber 
serum are falling — one has just landed on her skin and is mixing with the 
moisture (creating a beautiful wet-on-wet luminous effect), one is mid-air, 
one is still forming at the dropper tip.

Her skin is radiant — the serum and moisture create an almost glass-like 
reflective surface. You can see the light source reflected in the liquid on 
her skin.

Only her cheek, jaw, and the dropper are in frame. One eye barely visible at 
the top edge, with visible aegyo sal.

Background: Completely dissolved into warm creamy bokeh. One small glass serum 
bottle slightly visible as a blurred golden shape in the bottom-left corner.

Lighting: Warm golden sidelight from the left. The light catches every droplet 
and makes the serum glow.

Camera: 100mm macro, f/1.8, extreme shallow depth of field. The landing point 
of the serum on skin is the focus point. Everything else melts away.

Color palette: warm skin, liquid gold, amber, translucent.

COMPOSITION: Cheek and dropper fill the frame edge to edge. Thin margin at 
bottom for text.
```
