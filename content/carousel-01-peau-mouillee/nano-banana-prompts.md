## Workflow: Nano Banana → Canva

1. **Generate** all 7 images in Nano Banana using these prompts
2. **Review** each for brand consistency (warm tones, no cool blues, dewy skin)
3. **Export** at 1080 x 1350 px, PNG, maximum quality
4. **Upload** to Canva via `upload-asset-from-url` or manual upload
5. **Place** into the carousel design as background/main image per slide
6. **Add text overlays** per `copy-v2.md` — use Cormorant Garamond for headlines, DM Sans for body
7. **Add Sonagi watermark** bottom-right every slide (navy #1a2744, 60% opacity)
8. **Export** final carousel: PNG, high quality, all 7 slides
